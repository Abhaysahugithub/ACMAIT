Army Institute of Technology, Pune

The AIT ACM Student Chapter (AASC) is a student organization affiliated with the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), comprising driven and motivated individuals committed to personal and professional growth. Established in 2024, AASC is dedicated to nurturing both technical expertise and non-technical skills among its members, with the overarching goal of empowering individuals to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to the future landscape of computing and beyond.
